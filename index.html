    What we do:

    LAN Academy is a nonprofit committed to bringing high-quality education around the world through simple, cheap, and offline learning labs. These learning labs are established through partnerships between LAN Academy and locally-active non-profits. Local partners provide a physical space, guidance, and mentorship to students. LAN Academy provides our "Magic Box": a small offline server filled with the best educational content the world has to offer.

    Our Learning Labs bring the digital learning revolution to ones who need it the most - the 4.5 billion people in the world without internet access.

    Our Vision

    “Access to education is a crucial precondition to educational impact, but what matters most thereafter is the quality of education.” - USAID strategy (February 2011)

    We believe high-quality education is the best way to bring people out of poverty and that it is possible to do this on a global scale  by making the online abundance of quality educational materials accessible to underprivileged students through a worldwide network of partners who care about their local communities.

    At our heart, we embrace exploratory learning - the idea that it is possible for learning to feel natural and unforced by allowing students take control of their own learning. We believe that knowledge is rich and multidimensional and that different students learn in very different ways. The curiosity and unique interests of each student are what make our Learning Labs such a powerful education system.

    Why LAN Academy?

    Quality education is critical to realizing human potential, but schools in poverty-stricken regions are often ill-equipped, overcrowded, and unable to teach students beyond a basic level. NGOs working in these communities understand the need, but current education investment options are either costly or otherwise not ideal. New education technology such as MOOCs have potential, but most students who need educational intervention lack reliable internet access.

    LAN Academy is built for exactly these situations. Partner NGOs provide the physical space and lab supervision. We provide a fundraising campaign to cover the hardware costs. Our products and Learning Lab model allow the neediest students access to some of the best educators in the world.

    Our Impact

    Is this really an effective system? How do you know you are making an impact?

    Impact measurement is a concern for every socially-minded venture, especially for ones as ambitious as we are. We work continuously to perfect our education model, but from the very beginning we have based our efforts off the works of cutting edge education researchers such as TED Fellow Sugata Mitra, whose work can be seen this article he published in 2010 and in this  outline of his research.

    Our Learning Labs build off this research and are our attempt to answer the question: “But what about the students who do not have reliable internet access?” We think we are on the right track.

    As we expand, we will be constantly performing our own openly-available research on exactly how effective our specific setup is across countries and cultures.

    Current Partners:

    In 2012, we partnered with African Indian Alliance to install two labs in Karnataka, India. Field reports indicate that the current labs are popular and having an impact. We are in the midst of returning Summer 2013 to gather data, update labs, and install two additional labs in nearby villages.

    Visit the Collaborate With Us page to learn more and to express interest in becoming a new partner.
