About this pilot

Our goal in this pilot is to make learning World History as enjoyable and interesting as possible while also preparing students to get a score of 3 or higher on the AP World History Exam. Students will prepare for the exam by taking a computer-based World History course at their own pace. While the course itself is entirely digital, we will ask our students to show up at their designated physical computer lab three times/week for 1.5 hours sessions (Boston) or twice/week for 2.5 hr sessions (Voyagers/Acton). Our learning software and methodology are still experimental, so students will be asked to provide us feedback about the model. We will pay the AP World History Exam fee (a $90 value).

Who we are

LAN Academy Inc is a nonprofit start-up based out of Northeastern University and dedicated to providing world-class alternative education to students around the world, whether that's rural India, Micronesia, or Boston.

Goals of this course

  • To encourage student's interests and provide them a fascinating World History course
  • To prepare students to take and receive college credit from the AP World History test
  • To validate our personal learning paths as potential routes for more students to follow
  • To learn from our students how to improve our platform and content
