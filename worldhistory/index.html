About this pilot

Our goal in this pilot is to make learning World History as enjoyable and interesting as possible while also preparing students to do well enough to recieve college credit on the AP World History Exam. The pilot will consist of students learning through a computer-based World History course at their own pace. While the course itself is entirely digital, we will have our students show up to the same physical computer lab three times a week for 1.5 hours each time. Our learning software and methedology are still experimental, so we expect students to be giving feedback to our lab facilitators throught the time they are here. If a student wishes to take the AP World History test, we will be cover all their costs for that test.

Who we are

LAN Academy Inc is a Nonprofit based out of Northeastern University and dedicated to providing world-class alternative education to students around the world, whether that's rural India, Micronesia, or Boston.

Goals of this course

  • To encourage student's interests and provide them a fascinating World History course
  • To prepare students to take and receive college credit from the AP World History test
  • To validate our personal learning paths as potential routes for more students to follow
  • To learn from our students how to improve our platform and content
