Who we are

LAN Academy INC is an early-stage nonprofit based out of Northeastern and dedicated to bringing world-class secondary education to students in the developing world. They partner with organizations worldwide to turn their existing computer labs into offline digital tutoring labs using custom-built open source software and open educational resources. Working around the world, LAN Academy designs flexible, scalable, and offline solutions to give bright and motivated students the education they deserve regardless of their circumstances.

Previous Partnerships

LAN Academy started off as a partnership with African Indian Alliance to explore further uses of their computer lab. We are currently set up in two of their computer labs serving approximately seventy students. On our second trip to India, LAN Academy updated those labs with LAN Academy Version 1.5, and added a new lab in the Jesuit-run High School in Mundgod. In August of 2012 LAN Academy sent a prototype lab with volunteers to Micronesia as part of our efforts to pilot new methods of distribution.
